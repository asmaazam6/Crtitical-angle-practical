\# Critical Angle Practical Simulation



A 3D optics simulation built in Unity to demonstrate prism refraction and optical needle alignment.



\## Features

\* Raycasting-based light refraction physics.

\* Interactive optical needle alignment tools.

\* Real-time calculation of critical angles.

I draw the board and the paper, and I added the functionality of drawing onto the board. And then I made thumbpins, and I added the functionality of thumbpins and optical needles that are inserted into the board at some specific length and not fully inserted. Then I made prism and then I made it a glass prism diode, and I added functionality of rays that comes from the pins and passes, and the rays direct towards prism, and then I added the functionality of reflection through the prism to see the reflection image of the optical needle from the other side. Then we can in the same way we will be able to trace the critical angle of the glass prism.
